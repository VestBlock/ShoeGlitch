# Purpose
Use internal links to move authority and visitors toward booking.

# Use this skill when
- Adding related-page blocks
- Designing link suggestion helpers
- Updating page templates to cross-link intelligently

# Rules
- Link by service similarity, city similarity, and conversion depth.
- Always include at least one down-funnel link to `/book` or `/coverage`.
- Prefer a small number of high-quality links over giant lists.

# Suggested link clusters
- same service in other cities
- other services in the same city
- city hub
- booking flow
- coverage checker

# Anti-patterns
- Do not render repetitive city lists on every page.
- Do not link to routes that are thin or low quality.
- Do not rely on manual link curation when a helper can derive the right cluster.
