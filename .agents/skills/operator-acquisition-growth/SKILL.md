# operator-acquisition-growth

Use this skill when improving operator recruitment pages, operator funnels, or city-launch acquisition surfaces.

## Objective
Turn operator-intent traffic into:
- qualified applications
- city launch interest
- pickup/drop-off operator leads
- restoration and cleaning operator demand

## Rules
- Route operator traffic into the existing apply flow whenever possible.
- Use realistic language on territory and earnings.
- City pages must map to real market context.
- Operator pages are conversion surfaces, not generic hiring pages.

## Conversion modules to prefer
- pre-qualification card
- city opportunity summary
- role-specific CTA
- live-vs-coming-soon field clarity

## Anti-patterns
- inflated earnings claims
- fake launch urgency
- job-board style copy that ignores ShoeGlitch’s operating model
