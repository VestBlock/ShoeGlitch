# Page Section Blueprints

## Quick answer
One paragraph that answers the query directly in plain language.

## Long answer
Two to four paragraphs explaining process, fit, or local context.

## FAQ
- short answer first
- longer answer second

## CTA copy
- Book cleaning
- Start your order
- Check your coverage
- Compare restoration options

## Good copy traits
- specific service names
- real next step
- clear city mention
- easy for AI tools to quote
