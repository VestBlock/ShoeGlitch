# Page Template

## Hero
- H1
- one-sentence summary
- primary CTA
- secondary CTA

## Quick answer
- 2 to 4 sentence direct answer

## Summary bullets
- 3 to 5 bullets

## Long answer sections
- problem/context
- what matters most
- how ShoeGlitch approaches it
- pricing/timing/decision criteria
- next best step

## FAQ
- 4 to 6 questions

## Conversion block
- offer
- form or booking CTA
- trust cue

## Related links
- adjacent sneakers
- adjacent cities
- service pages

## Writing rules
- Start helpful, not clever.
- Answer the question before storytelling.
- Keep paragraphs short and quotable.
- Tie every section to a next action.
