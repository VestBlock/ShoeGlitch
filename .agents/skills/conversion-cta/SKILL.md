# Purpose
Make SEO pages earn revenue, not just visits.

# Use this skill when
- Adding CTA blocks
- Positioning booking buttons
- Designing quote or lead surfaces on city/service pages

# CTA rules
- One primary action above the fold.
- One support action for lower-friction visitors.
- Repeat the CTA after long-form content.
- Keep labels literal and action-oriented.

# Best ShoeGlitch CTA patterns
- `Start your order`
- `Check your coverage`
- `Compare services`
- `Mail in your pair`

# Layout rules
- keep CTA cards lightweight
- preserve mobile readability
- make trust and next-step clarity visible

# Anti-patterns
- Do not use vague labels like “Learn more” as the main CTA.
- Do not bury the booking path below FAQs only.
- Do not add popups or sticky UI that harms the booking form.
