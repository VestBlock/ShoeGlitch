# Purpose
Make local pages believable, useful, and conversion-ready.

# Use this skill when
- Building `/locations/[city]`
- Building city-specific service pages
- Adding service-area proof, ZIP coverage context, or market-specific CTAs

# City-page rules
- Use real city data from the app.
- Mention fulfillment options honestly: pickup, drop-off, or mail-in.
- Show service-area evidence where available.
- Tie the page back to active booking and coverage flows.

# Include
- city + state in title and H1
- service-area proof or market proof
- city-specific FAQ
- links to related services in the same city
- link to coverage checker

# Anti-patterns
- Do not fake neighborhood coverage.
- Do not claim same-day service unless the system supports it.
- Do not write city pages that ignore the actual fulfillment model.
